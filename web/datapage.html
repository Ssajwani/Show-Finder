Dataset from kaggle Various Anime Titles (16k), Reviews (130k) and Profiles (47k) from https://myanimelist.net/ Title of the anime, genre, number of episodes, scores, and synopsis We removed the following categories: Identification code of the anime, the popularity of the anime, and the amount of followers each anime has.
